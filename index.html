FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much will it cost to replace my windshield?

If you have a Florida insurance policy with full coverage, including comprehensive or glass coverage… your policy deductible will be waived for windshield replacement. This will result in absolutely no out of pocket expense for you. For auto glass other than the windshield, your deductible will apply. Your insurance company is required to waive your deductible in accordance with Florida Statute 627.7288 which states… Comprehensive coverage; deductible not to apply to motor vehicle glass. —The deductible provisions of any policy of motor vehicle insurance, delivered or issued in this state by an authorized insurer, providing comprehensive coverage or combined additional coverage shall not be applicable to damage to the windshield of any motor vehicle covered under such policy.

Is it true that I can only get one free windshield per year?

No. This is a common myth that we hear frequently. Per Florida Statute 627.7288 for Windshield Deductible requirements for insurance companies, your insurance company is required to not only waive your deductible for windshield replacement but if you carry comprehensive insurance, they are required to replace the glass as many times as necessary. Driving in Florida with a broken windshield is illegal and very unsafe. Your windshield is an integral part of your vehicles safety system.

You wouldn’t let your insurance company decide who your child’s pediatrician is… don’t let your insurance company decide who repairs your vehicle. Your insurance company is required to let you choose your own service provider.

What is OEM glass?

OEM stands for Original Equipment Manufacturer. This designation means that the manufacturer of the glass produces original auto glass for installation by the automobile manufacturer, during the vehicles assembly. Aftermarket glass is produced by a company that does not provide glass to the vehicles manufacturer. Aftermarket glass can differ from OEM glass in clarity, glass thickness, color and fit.
